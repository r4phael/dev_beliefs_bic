Dear Participant,

We are part of a research team with members from UFAL (Federal University of Alagoas), UFPE (Federal University of Pernambuco), UFPA (Federal University of Pará), UFCG (Federal University of Campina Grande), and PUC-RIO (Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio de Janeiro). We are studying the information needs to identify bug-introducing changes.

THE IDEA

We would like to collect the opinion of software developers/researchers about bug-introducing changes.

WHAT ARE BUG-INTRODUCING CHANGES?

During the process of development and evolution of software, developers perform changes, and these changes may introduce bugs, also known as bug-introducing changes [1].

[1] J. ́Sliwerski, T. Zimmermann, and A. Zeller, “When do changes induce fixes?” SIGSOFT Softw. Eng. Notes, vol. 30, no. 4, pp. 1–5, May 2005.[Online]. Available: http://doi.acm.org/10.1145/1082983.1083147
